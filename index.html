OUR THEME

Echoes of a Whisper isn’t about the loud, brash declarations that often dominate headlines. It’s about the quiet moments—the whispered ideas, the subtle shifts, the unnoticed actions—that ripple through the fabric of society, sparking revolutions, innovations, and movements. This year, we’re focusing on the kind of change that starts from a whisper. It’s the scientist working away in solitude, the artist whose work is quietly transformative, the activist whose cause begins with a single voice. The world has always been shaped by these quiet catalysts. Echoes of a Whisper is an invitation to explore these unseen forces that challenge the status quo, redefine the possible, and leave their mark long after the noise fades.

TEDxFISAT

In the world of TED, there are ideas, and then there are ideas worth spreading. TEDxFISAT is the local manifestation of this global ethos, bringing together a carefully curated roster of visionaries, innovators, and disruptors to spark conversations that matter. Each year, TEDxFISAT stands as a platform where groundbreaking ideas meet the people who make them happen, and 2025 will be no different. This year, TEDxFISAT returns with a theme that’s all about the hidden power of quiet revolutions: Echoes of a Whisper. It’s a nod to the voices that often go unheard—until they create waves that change the world.
